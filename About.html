About Hoot Hoot Online Store

What we are about

The Owls Store is an e-commerce site selling a wide range of products practically for everyone. We select the best sellers products and we offer them at really great prices. We take pride in what we do and strive to bring the best in all our products. If you are wondering why we are selling more products for owl and owl lovers, that's because we are a little more passionate about them. The Owls Store is for those who love owl-like merchandises and appreciates the intricate nature of the night owl. Browse through our shop and see them for yourself.



Lesedii Mak : CEO and Founder

Lesedii opened the store because she noticed that people were still very much afraid of owls because they have lived their lives believing that owls symbolize death. From ancient times on, owls have been linked with death, evil, and superstitions. Many cultures saw owls as a sign of impending death. So the goal is to show the population that owls are just as cute and fuzzy like dogs. :-)



Tom Hiddleston: Co-Founder


Tom decided to invest in the business because he is a big fan of owls and believes that owls are misunderstood and majestic.
